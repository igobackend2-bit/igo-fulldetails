OUR TECH PHILOSOPHY

Why Technology Changes Everything in Agriculture

Traditional farming relies on guesswork — when to water, when to fertilize, when to harvest. IGO Groups has replaced guesswork with data. Every farm we operate is monitored, measured, and optimised in real time using cutting-edge agricultural technology.

  • Real-time soil moisture and nutrient monitoring via IoT sensors
  • AI-powered early crop disease detection — 72 hours before visual signs
  • Drone surveillance for pest control and growth monitoring
  • Smart drip irrigation that reduces water usage by up to 40%
  • Farm automation modules that reduce manual labour dependency
TECHNOLOGY PILLARS

6 Technologies Powering Our Farms

Each technology is deployed at scale across IGO's farms, partner sites, and consulting projects across India.

📡

IoT Soil & Climate Sensors

Wireless sensor networks measure soil pH, moisture, temperature, and nutrient levels every 15 minutes. Data streams to the central dashboard for real-time decisions by the farm management team.

🤖

AI Crop Diagnosis

Our AI model — trained on 2M+ crop disease images — can detect 200+ diseases and deficiencies from drone imagery or a simple phone photo, enabling intervention weeks before traditional detection.

🚁

Drone Monitoring & Spraying

Drones survey fields daily, generate NDVI health maps, and perform precision aerial spraying of fertilizers and pesticides — reducing chemical usage by 35% vs. conventional spraying.

💧

Smart Drip Irrigation

AI-controlled drip systems irrigate based on real sensor data and weather forecasts — not fixed schedules. Water savings of 30–45% versus conventional methods are consistently achieved.

⚙️

Farm Automation Systems

Automated harvesting assist tools, conveyor systems, and remote-controlled machinery reduce farm labour dependency while maintaining quality and output consistency across large farm areas.

📱

India Green App

IGO's farmer-facing mobile platform provides personalised crop advice, market prices, farm diagnostics, weather alerts, and direct access to IGO's agronomy experts — in Tamil, Hindi, and English.

"Every polyhouse we build is a smart farm. Every irrigation line carries data as much as water."

— IGO Agritech Farms, Technology Division

🌡️

Climate-Controlled Polyhouses

1,000+ sq ft to 5-acre automated polyhouses with sensor-driven ventilation, shade nets, and irrigation — enabling year-round cultivation of high-value crops.

🌿

Hydroponic & Vertical Farming

Soil-less growing systems using nutrient-film and deep-water culture techniques — producing 6x more yield per square foot than conventional field farming.

📊

Data-Driven Agronomy

All farm data feeds into IGO's central analytics platform, generating farm health scores, yield forecasts, and corrective action reports for every plot under management.
