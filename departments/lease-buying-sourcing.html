IGO Lease & Buying Sourcing

Land leasing, property acquisition

ABOUT THIS DEPARTMENT

What IGO Lease & Buying Sourcing Does

This team handles land leasing and property acquisition for IGO's expanding farm and infrastructure footprint, working through due diligence, negotiation and documentation to secure the right sites for new ventures. They coordinate closely with Legal & Compliance and Engineering before any site is finalized.

KEY ACTIVITIES

What the Lease & Buying Sourcing Team Does

Land Identification & Shortlisting

Scouts and shortlists land parcels suitable for IGO's farming, infrastructure or commercial projects — evaluating soil quality, location, connectivity, water access and legal status before recommending sites.

Due Diligence & Title Verification

Conducts detailed legal and physical due diligence on every shortlisted site — verifying ownership, encumbrances, encroachments, survey records and regulatory clearances before any commitment is made.

Negotiation & Deal Structuring

Leads price negotiations with landowners and brokers, structuring lease or purchase terms that are commercially advantageous for IGO while being fair, transparent and legally sound.

Documentation & Registration

Manages the full documentation process for land transactions — from sale agreements and lease deeds through stamp duty, registration and mutation — coordinating with Legal & Compliance throughout.

Coordination with Engineering & Legal

Collaborates with the Engineering team for site feasibility assessments and with Legal & Compliance to ensure every acquisition is structurally viable and legally protected before finalisation.

Portfolio Management

Maintains a live register of all leased and owned land parcels across the group — tracking lease renewal dates, rent escalations, licence expiries and any boundary or encroachment disputes requiring attention.
