Our Story, Vision & Mission

Established in 2016. Building India's most trusted, technology-led agribusiness ecosystem.

WHO WE ARE

India's Farming-Tech Conglomerate

IGO Groups is a Chennai-headquartered agribusiness ecosystem operating 27 brands across 18 departments — spanning farm engineering, organic retail, fintech, trade, and agri-education. Since 2016, we've grown from a single crop-care brand into an integrated network connecting farms, food, finance, and technology across India. We are farmers, engineers, technologists, and entrepreneurs, united under one mission: putting genuine, technology-backed farming within reach of every Indian, from smallholders to institutional investors.

WHERE WE’RE HEADED

Our Vision & Mission

From This Building 27 Brands, 18 Departments, One Vision
Our Vision

To become India’s most trusted and innovative farming-tech brand, transforming every available space into sustainable and high-yield farming ecosystems.

To win lifetime loyal customers across pan-India by farming every square metre of fertile and non-fertile open land, indoor space, and rooftop space of buildings.

Looking ahead: IGO Group is expanding into new geographies and industries — from international organic exports to green energy — building toward a ₹500 Cr group revenue milestone and a truly pan-India farming-tech footprint.

Our Mission

IGO Agritech Farms’ mission is to create 1,000,000 Farmpreneurs across India — entrepreneurial farmers equipped with the land, technology, and training to build thriving farming businesses of their own.

Every Farmpreneur creates 5 direct jobs and 10 indirect jobs in their community — a multiplier effect that compounds into 15,000,000 jobs by 2036.

OUR JOURNEY

Company Milestones

Why we started: IGO Group was founded in 2016 by Dr. John Yesudhas on a simple observation — Indian farmers had the land and the will, but lacked reliable technology, fair market access, and genuine inputs. What began as a single crop-care brand grew, step by step, into the 27-brand ecosystem below.

2016

IGO Group founded with a singular vision for sustainable, enterprise-scale farming.

2017

Launched IGO Agritech Farms — the first brand under the conglomerate umbrella.

2019

Expanded to 10 brands across Core Agri and Retail & Food sectors.

2020

Launched the Tech & Digital vertical including IGO Farm Automation and IoT systems.

2022

Crossed 2,000 employees globally across 20+ brands and 15 operational departments.

2023

Launched Farmgate Mandi — digital farm gate buyback platform for transparent market access.

2024

India Green App launched as the central digital hub for the entire ecosystem.

2025

Expanded to 27 brands and 18 departments — targeting ₹500 Cr revenue milestone as India's fastest-growing farming conglomerate.
