WHO WE ARE

India's Most Comprehensive Farming Group

IGO Groups (India Green Organics) is a Chennai-headquartered diversified conglomerate operating across farming inputs, farm engineering, organic food retail, farming fintech, land investment, import-export trade, and precision farming technology. Founded in 2016 by Dr. John Yesudhas, we have grown from a single vision into a 26-brand powerhouse with 2,000+ global staff.

HOW WE BUILD IT

Technology at the Core

AI, automation, and connected systems running underneath every brand — not a pilot program, the way we actually operate.

AI & Diagnostics

AI-driven crop diagnostics and yield prediction across every polyhouse and open-field site.

Farm Automation

Automated irrigation, climate control, and nutrient dosing running on scheduled and sensor-triggered logic.

IoT Sensors

Soil moisture, temperature, and humidity sensors feeding live data into the India Green App.

Drone Monitoring

Aerial crop surveys and site inspections replacing manual walk-throughs across large-acreage projects.

Custom ERP

One integrated platform running finance, operations, HR, inventory, and CRM across all 26 brands.

R&D Pipeline

Continuous field pilots turning new crop varieties and farming methods into scalable IGO practice.
