WHO WE ARE

India's Most Comprehensive Agribusiness Group

IGO Groups (India Green Organics) is a Chennai-headquartered diversified conglomerate operating across agri-inputs, farm engineering, organic food retail, agri-fintech, land investment, import-export trade, and precision agritech. Founded in 2016 by Dr. John Yesudhas, we have grown from a single vision into a 26-brand powerhouse with 2,000+ global staff.
