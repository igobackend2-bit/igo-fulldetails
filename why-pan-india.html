The IGO Advantage

Rooted Across India.
Reaching the World.

IGO Groups has built a physical and operational presence spanning multiple states across India — delivering agritech services, organic retail, and farm solutions where farmers need them most, while exporting to 18+ countries globally.

OUR FOOTPRINT

Why Physical Presence Matters in Agriculture

Farming cannot be managed remotely. Soil condition, weather variability, farmer trust, and harvest logistics all require boots on the ground. IGO Groups has built state-wise operational teams, regional farm hubs, and local supplier relationships — making us a truly embedded national agribusiness player.

  • Active farm operations and consulting across 4 South Indian states
  • Regional procurement hubs for direct farmer sourcing
  • Valluvam and IGO Mart retail presence in multiple cities
  • APEDA-certified export operations from Chennai port
  • 50,000+ farmers connected across the national network
STATE OPERATIONS

Where We Operate Across India

IGO Groups has established operational infrastructure and farmer networks across these key agricultural states.

📍 Tamil Nadu

Headquarters & Primary Operations

IGO Groups is headquartered in Chennai (Uthandi Kanathur). Tamil Nadu is our core state — home to the largest concentration of farm projects, polyhouse installations, and retail outlets (Valluvam, Palm Cafe, Protein Cuts).

HQ — Chennai Polyhouse Farms Retail Outlets Export Hub
📍 Karnataka

Agritech Expansion Zone

Karnataka's diverse agro-climatic zones make it ideal for IGO's hydroponic and vertical farming installations. Key projects include polyhouse clusters in and around Bengaluru, Tumkur, and Hassan regions.

Hydroponic Farms Consulting Projects Nursery Supply
📍 Kerala

Organic & Spice Farming Hub

Kerala's organic farming tradition aligns perfectly with IGO's Valluvam organic retail brand. IGO sources certified organic produce, spices, and coconut products from Kerala-based farmer groups for national distribution.

Organic Sourcing Spice Procurement Farmer Networks
📍 Andhra Pradesh

Large-Scale Farm Engineering

Andhra Pradesh's large land parcels and irrigation-friendly geography are ideal for IGO's farm engineering and drip irrigation projects. IGO has completed multiple large-scale polyhouse and drip system installations in the region.

Farm Engineering Drip Irrigation Large-Scale Projects
