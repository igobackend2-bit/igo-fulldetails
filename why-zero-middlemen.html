The IGO Advantage

Zero Middlemen.
100% Value.

IGO Groups connects farms directly to consumers, retailers, and global export partners — eliminating every unnecessary layer and ensuring maximum value flows to the farmer and the buyer.

OUR APPROACH

Why We Removed the Middlemen

In India's traditional agri supply chain, produce passes through 6–8 intermediaries before reaching the consumer. Each layer adds cost, reduces freshness, and strips value from both the farmer and the buyer. IGO Groups was built from the ground up to eliminate this inefficiency entirely.

  • Farmers earn 30–45% more per unit sold through IGO's direct channels
  • Consumers receive fresher produce at fair market prices
  • Export buyers get directly-certified produce with full traceability
  • Zero commission, zero mark-up from hidden intermediaries
  • Complete supply chain ownership — from farm to final delivery
HOW IT WORKS

The Direct Chain — 4 Steps

From cultivation to delivery — every step managed, owned, and optimised by IGO Groups.

1

Farm & Cultivation

IGO Agritech Farms and partner farmers cultivate produce using precision farming. Every farm is monitored, certified, and quality-controlled at source.

2

IGO Processing Hub

Produce passes through IGO's own post-harvest handling, grading, cleaning, and packaging units — no third-party processors involved.

3

Direct Distribution

IGO's logistics network delivers directly to IGO Mart outlets, Valluvam organic stores, corporate clients, restaurants, and export warehouses.

4

Consumer & Export

End customers — whether a household in Chennai or a retailer in Dubai — receive traceable, farm-fresh produce at transparent pricing.

"When the farmer earns more and the consumer pays less — that is when we know our model is working."

— Dr. John Yesudhas, Founder & CEO, IGO Groups

🌾

Farmer Empowerment

Direct pricing gives farmers full visibility into what their produce earns. No hidden deductions or delayed payments.

📦

Full Traceability

Every batch of produce carries a digital trail — farm location, harvest date, certifications, and quality grade.

🌍

Global Direct Export

IGO Exports ships APEDA-certified produce directly to buyers across Middle East, Europe, and SE Asia — no trading intermediaries.
