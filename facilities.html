INSIDE IGO GROUP

Office Facilities

A look inside our Chennai head office — the workspaces, meeting rooms, and infrastructure that support our 18 departments every day.

OUR WORKSPACE

Built for Focused, Collaborative Work

Our head office at Uthandi Kanathur, Chennai, houses the teams behind all 18 IGO departments under one roof — from Agri Operations and Engineering to IT & AI and Finance. The space is organised around open collaboration, quick cross-department coordination, and the day-to-day realities of running a fast-moving agri-tech conglomerate.

Our Farm Campus from Above

An aerial view of the solar array, greenhouses, and fields surrounding our operations.

FACILITIES

What Our Office Offers

Reception & Head Office Building

Our Chennai head office building at Uthandi Kanathur is the front door for visitors, partners, and new team members joining IGO Group.

Open-Plan Workstations

Department teams sit in open, collaborative layouts that keep communication fast between Agri Operations, Engineering, Marketing, IT & AI, and every other vertical.

Team Collaboration Areas

Informal spaces where cross-functional teams huddle for quick problem-solving — a big part of how a multi-department conglomerate like IGO stays coordinated.

IT & Digital Infrastructure

Every workstation is connected into IGO's ERP, CRM, and MIS systems, managed by the IT & AI department, so teams can pull real-time data across departments.

A Home for New Talent

From interns to department heads, the office is designed to bring new hires up to speed quickly alongside experienced teams across all 18 departments.
