Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions about our services, products, and processes.

How do I apply for an IGO Farm Loan?

You can apply directly through our IGO Farm Loans portal or contact our Fintech division. We require basic land records and KYC to process NABARD/KCC subsidies.

What is included in Polyhouse construction?

Our turnkey polyhouse services include design, structural engineering, automated climate control, drip irrigation setup, and initial agronomy consulting.

Do you export organic produce globally?

Yes, our trade division exports certified organic fruits, vegetables, and millets to over 18 countries across the Middle East, Europe, and Southeast Asia.

How can I join the IGO team?

Check our Careers page for the latest openings! We are always looking for passionate agronomists, engineers, and supply chain experts.

Where is IGO Groups headquartered?

IGO Groups is headquartered at No. 17, Kovalan Street, 2nd Main Road, Uthandi Kanathur, Chennai – 600119, Tamil Nadu, India.

What is the SBASM Technology developed by IGO?

SBASM (Soil-Based Agri-Science Management) is IGO Group's proprietary farming methodology that optimises soil health, water usage, and crop yield using data-driven inputs. It forms the backbone of all IGO managed farm projects.

How many brands does IGO Groups operate?

IGO Groups operates 26 brands spanning farming-tech, farm engineering, retail, fintech, education, and sustainability sectors. You can view all brands on our Business Divisions page.

How can I invest in IGO managed farmland?

IGO Farmlands Estates offers managed farmland investment packages for individuals and NRIs. Contact our team at bd2@igogroups.com or visit our IGO Farmlands Estates page for details.

Does IGO Groups offer franchise opportunities?

Yes. Through IGO Franchise (FICO), we offer franchise models for agri-marts, polyhouse ventures, farm loan centres, and more. Visit our FICO page or contact us to learn more.

What crops are suitable for polyhouse farming?

Capsicum, tomato, cucumber, gerbera, rose, and strawberry are among the most profitable polyhouse crops in India. IGO Tech Farming Scientists can guide crop selection based on your region, soil, and investment size.

How can I contact the HR / Careers team?

You can reach our HR team directly at hr.admin@igogroups.com or submit your application via the Careers page.

What are IGO Groups' office hours?

Our head office is open Monday to Saturday, 10:00 AM to 7:30 PM IST. We are closed on Sundays and public holidays.

Does IGO export farm products internationally?

Yes. IGO Exports & Imports handles international trade of premium Indian farm produce. Our upcoming brand India Green Organics will focus specifically on certified organic exports to EU and USA markets.

Is there an app for IGO Group services?

Yes. The India Green App is IGO Group's unified digital platform providing farmers and investors access to all 26 brands' services including produce buying, farm loans, expert consultation, and market prices in one app.

Call us at +91 73977 89803, email bd2@igogroups.com, or use the Contact form on our website. You can also WhatsApp the same number.
