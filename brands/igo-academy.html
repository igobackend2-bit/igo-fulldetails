About IGO Academy

IGO Academy is the educational arm of IGO Group, operating from a 20+ acre live farm campus in Chennai since 2015. It delivers specialized, hands-on training in modern farming techniques, farming-tech entrepreneurship, and farm business management — taught by practicing agritech professionals across both classroom and on-field formats.
