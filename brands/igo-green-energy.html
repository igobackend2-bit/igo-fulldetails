About IGO Green Energy

IGO Green Energy will develop and deploy renewable energy infrastructure — primarily solar and small-scale wind — across farming zones in India. Designed to power polyhouses, cold storage units, and farm automation systems, the division will make IGO Group farms energy-independent and carbon-neutral.
